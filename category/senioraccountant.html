Senior Accountant

Banco De Oro - Quezon Branch

Quezon Avenue, Iligan City Full-time ₱75K-₱95K

Company Overview

Banco De Oro (BDO) is one of the Philippines’ largest and most trusted banks, known for its commitment to service excellence and financial innovation. With an extensive network of branches and digital banking services, BDO empowers individuals and businesses to achieve financial goals. Our culture is built on integrity, customer focus, and professional growth—making BDO a dynamic workplace for top-tier talent.

Job Description

We are seeking a Senior Accountant to join our dynamic finance department. You will play a key role in overseeing financial reporting, ensuring regulatory compliance, and supporting strategic decision-making through accurate financial data.

  • Manage month-end and year-end close processes
  • Prepare financial statements in compliance with GAAP
  • Coordinate audits and liaise with external auditors
  • Ensure tax compliance and assist with filings
  • Support budgeting and forecasting activities
  • Provide mentorship to junior accounting staff
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams on financial planning

Required Experience

  • 7+ years of professional accounting experience
  • In-depth knowledge of GAAP and financial reporting
  • Experience with accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks, NetSuite, SAP)
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • CPA certification preferred
  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
  • Excellent attention to detail and communication skills

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and annual bonus potential
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) matching
  • Hybrid work options
  • Professional development and CPA support
  • Generous paid time off and holidays
